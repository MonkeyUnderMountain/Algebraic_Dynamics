   \chapter*{Preface}
   \addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Preface}

   This document provides an introduction to algebraic dynamics, 
   focusing on the study of dynamical systems defined by algebraic maps on algebraic varieties. 
   The main interesting objects are \((X,f)\), 
   where \(X\) is an algebraic variety over a finitely generated field \(\kk\) of characteristic zero 
   and \(f: X \to X\) is a dominant rational self-map defined over \(\kk\).
